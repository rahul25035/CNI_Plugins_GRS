=== NOTE ON CILIUM RESOURCE USAGE ===
Cilium runs an agent (daemonset) on every node to manage eBPF programs.
The cilium-operator runs once cluster-wide for coordination.
eBPF programs run in kernel space - their CPU cost appears under the kernel,
not directly under the cilium pod. This makes overhead harder to measure
purely from pod resource requests.
